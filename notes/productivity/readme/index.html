Suggested Reading Order

For someone targeting Principal/Staff Engineer (L6/L7) roles, this sequence builds a durable productivity system:

  1. Parts 00–02: Foundations, self-management, and goal setting.
  2. Parts 03–04: Time management and deep work.
  3. Parts 05–06: Personal knowledge management and accelerated learning.
  4. Part 11: Engineering productivity practices.
  5. Part 08: Decision-making frameworks.
  6. Part 15: Designing a personal operating system that scales with senior leadership responsibilities.

This progression aligns productivity with technical depth, strategic thinking, and long-term career growth rather than focusing solely on completing more tasks.
